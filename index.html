About

idunhaveastudygram is an independent writing project focused on interdisciplinary inquiry across law, history, and the humanities.

The site features essays and reading reflections on legal history, selected works in music history, as well as books related to history, psychology, and language. Rather than treating these fields as isolated disciplines, the project explores how ideas, texts, and cultural forms interact across time.

This blog is intended for readers interested in historically informed, critical engagement with texts, music, and legal thought. It also functions as a curated writing portfolio for graduate applications and professional use.

Occasional updates and reading notes are shared on Instagram .

All views expressed here are personal and do not represent any institution.
